HOW IT WORKS

Water flow Sensor

measure the water flow rate in the system
and helps ensure proper and continuous water circulation.

Solar Panels

convert sunlight into electricity to power the system.

Solar Controller

Regulates charging from the solar panel and protects the battery from overcharging or deep discharge.

Battery

Stores electrical energy so the system can work at night or without sunlight

Buck Converter LM2596

Steps down voltage to power low-voltage components in the system.

ESP32 Development Board

Controls the system and manages communication between components.

LCD 16x2 with I2C

Displays system information and status to the user.

Solenoid Valve

Electrically controlled valve that opens or closes to control water flow.

channel relay

Acts as a switch controlled by the ESP32 to turn high-power devices (like the valve) on or off

Ultrasonic Sensor

Measures water level in the tank.

Soil Moisture Sensor

Detects soil moisture levels to determine watering needs.

Buzzer Sensor

Provides audible alerts for system status or errors.

Fuse

Protects the system from electrical overloads.

Diode

Prevents backflow of current in the circuit.

Switch

Manually controls the power supply to the system.
